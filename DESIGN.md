# ReliqueX Global Design System (DESIGN.md)

## 1. Aesthetic Ethos: 'Digital Heritage' Museum
The platform must evoke the feeling of a premium, high-tech museum archive. 
- **Dark Mode Default:** Backgrounds are deep `#050505` with `#e5e5e5` technical off-white foregrounds.
- **Accents:** Gold (`#d4af37`) is used sparingly for institutional authority, links, and highly critical highlights.

## 2. Typography Rules
- **Font Pairing:** 
  - **Headings (`font-heading`):** Use Inter (Geometric Sans-Serif) for all H1, H2, and H3 elements to establish authority.
  - **Body (`font-sans`):** Use Geist for standard prose to maximize readability.
  - **Code/Technical (`font-mono`):** Use Geist Mono for timestamps, metadata, citations, and inline code.
- **Vertical Rhythm:** 
  - Body text must use a line-height of `1.6` (`leading-[1.6]`) to create breathing room.
  - Subheadings must have significant top margin (`mt-12` or `mt-16`) and bottom margin (`mb-6` or `mb-8`) to separate technical concepts.
- **Column Constraints:** Limit text containers to `max-w-3xl` with `mx-auto` centering to prevent excessive line lengths, optimizing the reading experience for both humans and AdSense crawlers.

## 3. Highlighting Technical Keywords
To emphasize the platform's E-E-A-T (Expertise), core architectural milestones (e.g., *GPT-4*, *Claude 3*, *LLaMA*) should be visually elevated within the body text.
- **Implementation:** Wrap keywords in a subtle gold background (`bg-accent/15`) with a thin border (`border-accent/20`) and monospace tracking (`font-mono tracking-tight`).

## 4. Trust Signals (E-E-A-T)
- Every generative asset must be explicitly marked with a **C2PA Verification Badge**.
- Long-form editorial content must exceed 1,500 words and conclude with a **Technical Bio** detailing the 'ReliqueX Curatorial AI' persona.
